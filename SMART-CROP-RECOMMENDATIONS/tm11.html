ABOUT THE PROJECT

Project Overview

This Smart Crop Recommendation system is a full-stack application designed to assist farmers in making informed decisions about crop selection. By inputting environmental factors such as soil nutrient levels (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium), temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall, the system leverages a machine learning model to predict the most suitable crop for those specific conditions.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Challenge: Ensuring model accuracy with diverse data.
    Solution: We selected a Random Forest model due to its robustness and high accuracy. We also preprocessed the data carefully to handle any inconsistencies.
  • Challenge: Creating an intuitive user interface.
    Solution: Streamlit was chosen for the app interface for its simplicity, allowing us to build an interactive dashboard with minimal code. The landing page was designed to be visually engaging and easy to navigate.

Tools & Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python, Streamlit
  • Machine Learning: Scikit-learn (RandomForestClassifier), Pandas, NumPy
  • Data Visualization: Chart.js, Matplotlib
  • Deployment: Localhost server via Streamlit

Plant Gallery

Plant Details

Chickpea

The chickpea is a versatile legume known for its nutty taste and high protein content. It's a key ingredient in dishes like hummus and curry.

Climate & pH: Chickpeas thrive in moderately warm summers with daytime temperatures between 70°F and 80°F. They prefer well-drained, loamy or sandy soils with a pH between 6.0 and 7.0.

Coffee

Coffee is a globally popular brewed drink made from roasted coffee beans. It is cherished for its stimulating effects and rich aroma.

Climate & pH: Coffee plants prefer a mild, tropical climate with temperatures between 60°F and 70°F. They grow best in well-drained, volcanic soils with a pH of 5.0 to 6.0.

Kidney Beans

Named for their shape, kidney beans are a variety of the common bean. They are rich in fiber and are a staple in cuisines worldwide, especially in chili.

Climate & pH: Kidney beans are a warm-weather crop, growing best in temperatures between 65°F and 85°F. The ideal soil is well-drained with a pH between 6.0 and 7.0.

Jute

Jute is a natural fiber derived from plants. It's used to make burlap, hessian, or gunny cloth and is known for being affordable and eco-friendly.

Climate & pH: Jute grows best in a warm, humid climate with temperatures between 75°F and 95°F. It thrives in loamy alluvial soils with a pH between 6.0 and 7.5.
